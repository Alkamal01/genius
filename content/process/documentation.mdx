# Documentation — What you must submit

## Short Intro

Teams upload to Drive; Hub will publish. To be accepted, every submission must include:

## Required Items (checklist)

* **submission.md** — fully filled (use template)

* **README.md** — team contacts & short summary

* **assets/** — minimum 3 photos + captions + consent

* **assets-index.md** — list of files and captions

* **video demo link** OR Vercel preview link

* **consent files** for identifiable people

## Naming Rules

YYYYMMDD_type_shortdesc (example: 20251125_photo-market-entrance.jpg)

## File Size Rule

Videos >50MB must be on Drive / YouTube (link in assets folder).

## PR & Review Flow

Kamal creates branch, copies files, opens PR, reviewer checks checklist, merges. Hub lead or organiser merges final PR.
