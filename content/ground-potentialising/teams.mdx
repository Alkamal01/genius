# Team: [Team Name] — Discovery Log & Submission Draft

## Sections

* **Team contact & short summary** (1–2 lines)

* **Problem statement** (2–3 sentences) — who, where, why it matters

* **Evidence list** (photos, audio, video links with captions & consent)

* **Research notes** (who was interviewed, dates, sample size)

* **Preliminary solution idea / prototype description**

* **Tests done** (if any) and quick results (numbers, observations)

* **Next steps and support needed** (mentorship, equipment, funds)

## Example

Problem: Market waste blocks drains at Zaria Central Market. Evidence: 5 photos, 2 interviews (audio transcripts attached), local estimate: 30kg/day of organic waste. Prototype: test a decentralized collection drum at one block for 2 weeks.
