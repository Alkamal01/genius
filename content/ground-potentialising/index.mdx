## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Effective Field Research

### Before You Go

- **Research the context** â€” What's already known about this problem?
- **Prepare questions** â€” Open-ended, not yes/no
- **Bring consent forms** â€” Get permission before recording
- **Go with a partner** â€” Safer and you can compare notes

### During Your Visit

- **Observe first** â€” Spend 15-30 minutes just watching
- **Ask "why" and "how"** â€” Not just "what"
- **Record exact words** â€” Don't paraphrase quotes
- **Take contextual photos** â€” Show the place, not just close-ups
- **Note what surprises you** â€” Assumptions that were wrong

### After Your Visit

- **Upload immediately** â€” Don't lose photos or forget details
- **Reflect honestly** â€” What did you learn? What confused you?
- **Share with team** â€” Discuss patterns and insights
- **Follow up if needed** â€” Sometimes you need a second visit
