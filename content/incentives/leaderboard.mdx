## ðŸ“Š Understanding the Rankings

### How Ranks Are Calculated

**Teams:**
- Ranked by total team points
- Team points = sum of all team activities
- Individual member contributions count toward team total

**Individuals:**
- Ranked by individual points only
- Individual sensing entries, personal uploads
- Does not include team points

### Tie-Breaking Rules

If two teams/individuals have the same points:

1. **Quality of work** (judges review submissions)
2. **Submission time** (earlier is better)
3. **Diversity of activities** (points from multiple categories > all from one)

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Why don't I see my points yet?**  
A: Check the update schedule. Points are added daily at 6 PM. If it's been longer, request a correction.

**Q: Can I see other teams' point breakdowns?**  
A: Yes, the sheet is public. You can see how everyone earned their points.

**Q: Do points from before I joined a team count?**  
A: Individual points stay with you. Team points only count activities done as a team.

**Q: What if I leave a team?**  
A: Your individual points stay with you. Team points stay with the team.

**Q: Can points be removed?**  
A: Only for plagiarism or ethics violations. Otherwise, points are permanent.
