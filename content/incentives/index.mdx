## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I earn points as an individual or only as a team?**  
A: Both! Individual sensing entries earn individual points, team submissions earn team points.

**Q: What if I'm on multiple teams?**  
A: Points are tracked separately for each team. Individual points stay with you.

**Q: How are ties broken?**  
A: Quality of work, then submission time (earlier is better).

**Q: Can I lose points?**  
A: No, but plagiarism or ethics violations result in disqualification.

**Q: When are prizes distributed?**  
A: After each event (Refinery, Finale) and final winners announced at closing ceremony.
