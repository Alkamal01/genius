# Incentives & Leaderboard

## Intro Paragraph

We give points for participation to encourage action: research uploads, PR drafts, presentations and wins.

## How Points Are Earned (example)

* Intake + evidence (3 photos + 1 paragraph): **10 points**

* PR opened (draft): **20 points**

* Presented at Midway (The Refinery): **15 points**

* Final winner (hub level): **30 points**

## How Prizes Are Awarded

Hub judges award local prizes after events; prizes range from cash vouchers to mentorship packages.
