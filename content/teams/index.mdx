### Stage 1: Ground ðŸ”

- Field research and problem discovery

**Deliverables:**

**Deliverables:**
- Team discovery log
- Clear problem statement (who, what, where, why)
- Preliminary solution idea
- Updated `submission.md`

**Duration:** 3-5 days

- Updated team discovery log
