## T-Medic

## The System

### Hybrid Storage
| Layer | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| Off-chain DB | Accessibility, privacy |
| On-chain Hash | Truth, immutability |

### Certify on Cardano
*   One-click wallet signature
*   Hash embedded in transaction metadata
*   Small ADA fee anchors permanently

### Tamper Flagging
*   Automatic comparison
*   Current record vs. blockchain fingerprint
*   Mismatch = immediate invalidation

### Verification Link
*   Generated after certification
*   Anyone can audit on Cardanoscan
*   Public proof of integrity
