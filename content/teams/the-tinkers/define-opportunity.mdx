# The Tinkers — Define Opportunity

## The Opportunity
Create a **new standard for medical truth**—where patient safety is guaranteed by code, not just policy.

## The Problem Scale
*   **Millions** of paper records at risk
*   **No audit trail** for most healthcare systems
*   **Easy forgery** of medical documents
*   **Patient safety** compromised

## The Unlock
| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Trust the administrator | Verify via blockchain |
| Paper burns | Hash is forever |
| Tampering undetected | Immediate flagging |
| Fragmented records | Verifiable history |

## Who Benefits
*   **Patients:** Safety guaranteed
*   **Hospitals:** Proof of integrity
*   **Insurance:** Fraud reduced
*   **Regulators:** Easy auditing

## Vision
> "A digital notary for medical truth, secured by Cardano."
