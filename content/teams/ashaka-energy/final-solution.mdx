# Ashaka Energy — Final Solution

## Energy Commons Protocol

**"Powering dignity through visibility: A community-operated energy stewardship system."**

## The System

### Community Reporting Tool
*   Simple mobile-web interface
*   Log outages and infrastructure risks
*   "Problem spotted" quick reporting

### Stewardship Rewards
*   Energy Steward Tokens minted for participation
*   Gamifies maintenance and vigilance
*   Creates community "immune system"

### Visibility Dashboard
*   Real-time grid status for leaders
*   Coordinate usage during power windows
*   Protect vulnerable infrastructure

## Technical Stack
*   **Laravel/PHP** — Backend
*   **Cardano** — Metadata/Smart Contracts for trust
*   **Mobile-Web** — Frontend interface

## Live Status
✅ Pilot ready for 50 users in Ashaka
✅ Governance transition planned

## Key Achievement
> Successful validation of user reports to Cardano verification.

## The Big Lesson
> "We redefined the 'energy problem' as a 'governance problem.' By empowering youth to be guardians of the grid, we're solving vandalism from the bottom up."
