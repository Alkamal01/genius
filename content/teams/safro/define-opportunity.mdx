# Safro — Define Opportunity

## 1. Team Identity & Mission

**Team Name:** Team Safro
**Project Name:** Safro Connect
**Tagline:** "Building the trust layer for the informal economy: From Zaria to the Northern Corridor."

**Our Shared Mission:**
We are a collective of Zaria natives—developers and researchers—united by a single belief: **Trade should be safe for everyone.** Whether you are using a smartphone in Lagos or a feature phone in Sabon Gari, our goal is to protect the livelihoods of the informal traders who sustain our nation's food supply.

**Project Category:** RealFi, Supply Chain, Financial Inclusion, Regenerative Tech

## The Opportunity
Our goal is to build the **trust layer for the informal economy**, connecting the physical markets of Zaria to the digital security of the blockchain.

### Market Context
*   **₦2.5 Trillion** — Estimated value of informal trade in Northern Nigeria.
*   **72%** — Percentage of traders who have reported losses due to fraud.
*   **85%** — Traders relying on basic feature phones.
