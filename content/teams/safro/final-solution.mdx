## Governance & Accountability
We recognize that relying solely on code or AI for vulnerable populations creates risk. Therefore, Safro Connect employs a **Hybrid Governance Model** to ensure safety and fairness.

### 1. The "Invisible Guardian" (Smart Contract)
*   **Role:** Automated escrow via Cardano Plutus contracts.
*   **Function:** Ensures funds are tamper-proof and safe from volatility (via Stablecoin conversion).
*   **Safety:** Funds are locked programmatically, meaning no individual can steal or mismanage them.

### 2. Human-Centric Dispute Resolution
In the event of a dispute, such as goods not being delivered, the decision is not made by an isolated algorithm.

*   **Tier 1:** AI analyzes transaction patterns to flag potential anomalies *before* locking funds.
*   **Tier 2:** Actual disputes are routed to a **Human Mediator**. Initially, this is the Safro Team, but it will evolve into a Community DAO of trusted market leaders.

**Why this matters:** We prioritize fairness. This ensures that traders who may struggle with digital systems always have a human voice in the process.

## Regenerative Impact

### Identity Building
Beyond simple payments, this system creates **Financial Identity**. By recording valid transactions on-chain (via Atala PRISM), we turn a trader's history into a "Trust Score."

**The Result:** This unlocks future access to credit for the unbanked, transforming "invisible" trade into visible, bankable assets.
