## From Markets to Code

Our journey began in the dusty lanes of Sabon Gari market in Zaria. We arrived with a simple assumption: traders needed a faster payment app. We were wrong. By sitting on mats with tomato dealers and sharing tea with union leaders, we discovered that **speed was not the problem. Fear was.**

Traders like Malam Musa were rejecting large orders from distant buyers because they had been burned by fraud before. This "Trust Deficit" was freezing capital and keeping trade hyper-local.

### The Pivot
Realizing that a smartphone-only app would exclude the majority of our users, we pivoted to a **Dual-Interface Ecosystem**. We stopped trying to modernise the market and instead built a bridge to it. We developed a USSD gateway for feature phones (Nokia 3310s) and a mobile app for digital natives, ensuring that everyone could participate in the trust economy.

## Achievements & Reflections

Team Safro's most profound achievement was not just the code, but the realization that **technology must bend to culture, not the other way around.**

*   **Key Milestone:** Successfully triggering a blockchain escrow contract via a basic USSD interaction in the Hausa language.
*   **The Big Lesson:** We built a bridge, translating complex blockchain trust into a language Zaria understood.
