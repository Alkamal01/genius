# MubaXpress — Define Opportunity

## The Opportunity
Revive dormant campus infrastructure to create a **trusted, decentralized economy** for student commerce.

## Market Size
*   **15,000+ students** at ABU Zaria
*   **₦50M+** in used textbook transactions annually
*   **Zero** trusted verification system

## The Unlock
By combining Post Office + Blockchain:
*   **University wins:** Revitalized infrastructure, engaged students
*   **Students win:** Safe commerce, verified trust
*   **Planet wins:** Circular economy for textbooks

## Regenerative Value
Unlike platforms that extract fees, MubaXpress **circulates value**:
*   Physical deliveries build on-chain reputation
*   Reputation unlocks future opportunities
*   Graduates carry their trust score forward
