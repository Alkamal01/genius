## ðŸ¤ How to Join a Pod

### Step 1: Fill the Pod Sign-Up Form

**[Pod Sign-Up Form](https://tally.so/r/xXV5NG)**

**What to include:**
- Your name and contact (WhatsApp/Email)
- **Skills you can offer:**
  - Technical: Frontend, Backend, Mobile, Data, etc.
  - Non-technical: Research, Writing, Design, Testing, etc.
- **Problem areas you're interested in:**
  - Agriculture, Health, Education, Waste, Transport, Finance, etc.
- **Time commitment:**
  - Hours per week you can dedicate
  - Availability for meetings

**Pro tip:** Be specific about your skills. "I can build React apps" is better than "I'm a developer."

---

### Step 2: Browse Open Pods

Check the [Active Pods list below](#-active-pods-recruiting) to see:
- What problem each pod is working on
- Current team members and their roles
- What skills they need
- How to contact them

---

### Step 3: Contact Pod Lead

**Two ways to join:**

1. **Direct contact:** Message the Pod Lead listed in the pod description
2. **Wait for invitation:** Pod leads will review sign-up forms and invite members

**What to say when contacting:**
> "Hi! I'm [Name], and I saw your pod [Pod Name] is working on [Problem]. I have experience in [Skill] and would love to join. I can commit [X hours/week]. Are you still looking for [Role]?"

---

### Step 4: Join Pod & Start Working

Once accepted:
- Join the pod's WhatsApp/Telegram group
- Attend the first team meeting
- Review the problem statement together
- Divide initial research tasks
- Start documenting your work

## ðŸ†• Start Your Own Pod

Don't see a pod working on your problem? **Start one!**

### How to Create a Pod

1. **Define your problem clearly:**
   - Who is affected?
   - What's the root cause?
   - Why does it matter?

2. **Do initial research:**
   - Interview 3-5 people affected
   - Gather basic evidence (photos, quotes)
   - Validate the problem is real

3. **Submit pod creation form:**
   - **[Create New Pod Form](https://tally.so/r/xXV5NG)**
   - Include problem statement
   - List your role and skills
   - Specify what roles you need

4. **Your pod will be listed here** within 24 hours
