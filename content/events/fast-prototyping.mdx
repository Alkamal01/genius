# EVENTS — Event 2: Fast Prototyping with AI (29 Nov 2025)

## Purpose

Practical workshop teaching how to build MVPs fast using AI & low-code tools.

## Title

Fast Prototyping with AI — 29 Nov 2025

## What it is

Hands-on workshop to help teams build quick prototypes (chatbots, simple web UI, data processing) in a day. Focus on the smallest testable idea.

## Agenda

* Intro to rapid prototyping principles (10 min)

* Tool overview: low-code/AI tools to try (15 min)

* Guided build session (60 min) — breakouts by team

* Show & Tell (20 min)

## Required for teams

Bring at least one clear problem statement and 3 photos / 1 user quote.
