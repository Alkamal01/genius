# Evaluation & Selection Process

## 3.1 The Gatekeeper: Evaluator Vetting
Before a team can cast a vote, they must prove they understand the principles.
*   **Who:** Two reps per team (Team Lead + Tech Lead).
*   **Requirement:** 100% on Regenerative Development & Hub Governance Test.
*   **Curriculum:** Regenerative Principles, Catalyst Criteria, Sociocracy 101.

## 3.2 The Philosophy
"Our Representatives, Not Our Rivals." We are One Hub. Your vote is a strategic decision for the success of the Bioregion.

## 3.3 The 4 Pillars of Evaluation
Teams use a Forced Ranking System (1â€“14) based on:
1.  **Ground Truth:** Is the problem real? Evidence of engagement?
2.  **Regenerative Potential:** Does it heal the system? Create positive loops?
3.  **Tech/Narrative:** Is the integration valid? Does the story match the problem?
4.  **Global Readiness:** Prepared for the world stage? Professional docs?

## 3.4 Voting Mechanics
1.  **Private Sheets:** Unique links.
2.  **No Self-Voting:** Prohibited.
3.  **Mandatory Rationale:** Written explanation required ("Proof of Thought").
4.  **External Audit:** Validated by PRISMA/WADA Global Evaluators.

## 3.5 Selection Timeline
*   **Vetting Test:** Dec 31st - Jan 2nd
*   **Voting Window:** Jan 3rd - Jan 4th
*   **Announcement:** Jan 6th
